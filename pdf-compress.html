Advanced PDF Size Reducer

Compress one or multiple PDFs to your target KB fully in browser. Free, fast, secure, and mobile-friendly.

Upload PDFs & Enter Target KB

Select one or more PDF files and enter target KB per file. Compression happens fully in your browser.

How It Works

  • Upload one or more PDFs from your device.
  • Enter the target size in KB.
  • Click Compress PDFs to compress all files approximately to target size.
  • Progress bar shows overall compression progress.
  • Once done, click Download All PDFs to get your compressed files.
  • All compression is fully local in your browser – complete privacy.
